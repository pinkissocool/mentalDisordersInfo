What is a Mood Disorder

Mood disorders are mental conditions that can drastically affect their mood.

Types of Mood Disorders Definition
Bipolar Disorder A.K.A. Manic-depressive, mood swings from extreme feelings of happiness and depression
Major Depressive Disorder A.K.A. Clinical Depression, extreme feelings of sadness that can last for long periods of time
